Etch-a-Sketch Color Game by AKIL

The instructions to play the Etch-a-Sketch (Color Game) in LAPTOP (Not for Mobile)..

1.Grid Size Selection:

  • Use the "SELECT THE NUMBER OF GRID" slider to choose the size of the grid.

2.Color Selection:

  • Use the "SELECT THE COLOR" color picker to choose the drawing color.

3. Drawing:

  • Click "START THE GAME" to generate the grid and start drawing.
  • Move your cursor over the grid cells to draw with the selected color.
  • Click "ERASE" to remove color from cells.
  • Use "ERASE ALL COLOR" to clear the entire grid Color.

4. Reset:

  • Click "RESET" to reload the page, clearing the grid and resetting settings.
